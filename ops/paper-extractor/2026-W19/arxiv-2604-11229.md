### One-line summary

RECIPER is a dual-view dense retrieval pipeline for materials-science procedural QA that indexes both raw paragraphs and LLM-extracted procedural summaries, yielding +3.73 Recall@1 and +2.85 nDCG@10 over paragraph-only retrieval across multiple backbones.
