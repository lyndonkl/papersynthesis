### Stated purpose and audience
Introduces SPD-RAG, a hierarchical multi-agent retrieval-augmented generation framework that decomposes a problem along the document axis (one sub-agent per document), and reports benchmark results on LOONG against Normal RAG and Agentic RAG baselines.
