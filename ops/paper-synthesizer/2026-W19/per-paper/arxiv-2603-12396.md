# Pass A — Within-paper layered summary

## 30,000 ft (paper's claim, one sentence)
Adds two test-time-only modifications to the Search-R1 agentic-RAG pipeline (a contextualization module + a retrieved-document deduplication module), lifting EM by 5.6% and cutting turns 10.5% on HotpotQA + Natural Questions.

## 3,000 ft (argument + evidence, 2-3 sentences)
This paper investigates test-time modifications to the iterative-search Search-R1 pipeline (Jin et al. 2025) to address two known shortcomings: redundant retrieval of previously-seen documents, and ineffective integration of retrieved content into the current generation prompt. The two components are a contextualization module (better integrates retrieved info into reasoning) and a de-duplication module (replaces previously-retrieved docs with the next most relevant). On HotpotQA and Natural Questions, the GPT-4.1-mini contextualization variant gives a 5.6% EM lift and 10.5% turn reduction over the Search-R1 baseline.
