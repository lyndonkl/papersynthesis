# Pass A — Within-paper layered summary

## 30,000 ft (paper's claim, one sentence)
Reframes the reranker's output API: instead of a scalar score, emit a contribution statement and an evidence-passage rewrite, so the agent receives only what's needed for the LLM context.

## 3,000 ft (argument + evidence, 2-3 sentences)
Prism-Reranker argues scalar rerankers force agents to dump whole documents into context. It trains a family of rerankers (0.8B/2B/4B/9B) on Qwen3.5 with a hybrid objective: pointwise distillation from a strong commercial reranker plus supervised fine-tuning on contribution and evidence-passage targets, with LLM-as-Judge ensembles of five frontier LLMs reconciling label noise. The same recipe extends Qwen3-Reranker-4B with contribution+evidence outputs while improving its average BEIR-QA NDCG@10 by +1.54 — a small but real lift, and weights+recipe+eval are released.

## 300 ft (specifics with hedging preserved, one sentence)
Reranker family (0.8B-9B) that outputs a contribution statement and an evidence-passage rewrite alongside the relevance verdict; +1.54 average NDCG@10 on BEIR-QA when applied to Qwen3-Reranker-4B.
