# Pass A — Within-paper layered summary

## 30,000 ft (paper's claim, one sentence)
Reframes OCR as a query-driven, on-demand parsing step inside visual-document RAG, decoupling retrieval granularity from rigid page-level chunks.

## 3,000 ft (argument + evidence, 2-3 sentences)
AgenticOCR positions itself as the 'third building block' of the visual-RAG stack, alongside embedding and reranking. The argument: page-level chunking and full-text OCR overload the generator's attention with extraneous context, and compressing visual tokens further increases hallucination risk. The system autonomously analyzes layout, identifies regions of interest, and selectively recognizes them — a 'thinking with images' approach that improves both efficiency and accuracy on long-document understanding tasks.
